# Zerobelow Redesign Direction

## New Aesthetic: Raw Digital Brutalism + High Contrast

**Core Philosophy:**
- Reject polish. Embrace raw, unfiltered energy.
- High contrast black/white with electric accent colors
- Asymmetric, intentionally "broken" layouts
- Heavy typography with irregular spacing
- Glitch effects, sharp angles, no rounded corners
- Feels like a real hackathon, not a corporate event

## Color System
- **Primary**: Pure black (#000000) and pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent 1**: Electric cyan (#00FFFF)
- **Accent 2**: Hot magenta (#FF00FF)
- **Accent 3**: Lime green (#00FF00)
- **Background**: Deep charcoal (#0A0A0A)

## Typography
- **Display**: Monospace (IBM Plex Mono, Space Mono) - all caps, bold, tight
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial) - minimal, direct
- **No serif fonts**, no decorative fonts
- Heavy use of text as design element

## Layout
- Overlapping elements
- Diagonal text rotations
- Asymmetric grids
- Large negative space with sudden dense clusters
- Borders and lines instead of shadows
- Raw, unpolished appearance

## Visual Elements
- Pixelated/glitchy effects
- Hand-drawn boxes and underlines
- Bold geometric shapes
- Stark photography (high contrast, dramatic)
- ASCII-inspired patterns
- No gradients, no blur, no soft edges

## Interaction
- Sharp, instant transitions (no easing)
- Hover states that invert colors
- Click feedback that feels mechanical
- Glitch effects on interaction
- No smooth animations

## Feeling
- Energetic, rebellious, authentic
- Tech-forward but raw
- Exciting and slightly chaotic
- Real, not polished
